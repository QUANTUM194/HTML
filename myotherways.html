Other Ways to stay Green

Plant a Tree

Planting a tree is something that anyone can do, and it will help to take CO2 out of our atmosphere, making the air quality around you better.

Recycle

Recycling things instead of throwing them only takes a few extra seconds of your day, but will help to make this world a better place.

Save Water

Something as simple as turning off the tap every time can save gallons of water over time, and not waste Earth's finite resources.
