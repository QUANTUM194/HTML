Why you should buy an EV

Electric Vehicles started mass producing in the 1970's, and have only got better since. With the rising gasoline prices, and carbon dioxide in the air, a cleaner alternative will be needed very soon. That alternative is an electric vehicle. While an EV may cost more than the average spender may want to spend on a car, you will gain back that money overtime, and be helping out the environment at the same time.

EV's only need about $14.40 to fill up completely, while a normal gasoline car will cost about $32.76 to fill up completely. This means that you will save about $18.36 every time that you recharge the car. This means that in 1 year, you will have saved about $706 per year. So depending on the electric vehicle that you buy, with enough time you will save a lot of money.
