Solar Energy

How solar panels work

Step 1)The Solar Panel
The Solar Panel absorbs the energy from the sun, and turns it into DC power

Step 2)The Inverter
The Inverter converts the DC to AC and controls the power supply

Step 3)The Breaker Panel
The Breaker Panel distributes the electricity throughout the housebr>
Step 4)Utility Meter
The Utility Meter lets the excess energy from the solar panel flow to the grid

Step 5)Utility Grid
The Utility Grid provides extra energy to the house if more is needed that the solar panels cannot already supply

Why you should buy solar panels

Buying some solar panels is a great idea. They will make majorly cut your electric bill, saving you money, and use clean energy at the same time.

A Watt is about $3-4, so if you purchased a 7.65kW system for your house(average energy needed per house), it would cost you about $16,350. But in 10 years, it will save you $1934! In 20 years, you will save $30,372!
